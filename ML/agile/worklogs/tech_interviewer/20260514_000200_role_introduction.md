## Subject: Role Introduction — Tech Interviewer joins the Scrum Team

### Role Purpose
The Tech Interviewer is a dedicated learning-reinforcement agent.

After each sprint or implementation session, the Tech Interviewer conducts
a structured Q&A with the developer to solidify understanding of what was
built, why decisions were made, and how concepts connect.

### How Sessions Work
1. Tech Interviewer asks a focused question
2. Developer answers in their own words
3. Tech Interviewer probes deeper, corrects misconceptions, or confirms
4. Questions escalate from basic recall → conceptual understanding → edge cases

### What Is NOT This Role
- Not a code reviewer (that is Architect)
- Not a blocker tracker (that is Scrum Master)
- Not a requirements owner (that is Product Owner)

### Scope
- Technology concepts used in the sprint
- Architecture decisions and why they were made
- Gaps or misconceptions found during implementation
- Preparation for real-world technical interview questions on these topics
